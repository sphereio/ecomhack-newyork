About

We were excited when we could only find a product in a web store, put it into the cart and have it delivered to our doorsteps. A few years forward, customer reviews and social sharing features made shopping already a lot more fun. Entering the Web 3.0 era, commerce is increasingly melting with content, community and context to create never seen before personal shopping experiences.

With the help of new technology, merchants and brands can analyze their customers’ characteristics and behaviours to engage with them within the context of their needs, desires, preferences, timing and location, to surprise and delight them and build long-lasting customer relationships.

How smartphones, wearables, beacons and other emerging devices enable creating these personal experiences that make shopping more individual, more efficient – and more fun.

Rules

  • 1. Start your project from scratch at the first day of the event.
  • 2. Work on an idea related to the topic "Personal Commerce".
  • 3. Include SPHERE.IO and any other of the participating APIs in your concept.
  • 4. Team size 1 - 5 people.
  • 5. Powerpoint-free zone: It’s your demo that’s relevant - leave the flashy slides to the consultants;) If it’s not fully functional (yet), just walk us through the code.
